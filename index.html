Mission: High-fidelity predictive modeling of complex materials under extreme conditions (high temperature, high stress, corrosive environment etc.) is crucial for accelerating material design and optimization to address the pressing challenges in our world. This research group aims to leverage both fundamental and use-inspired AI research, coupled with cutting-edge experiments, to revolutionize and transform traditional materials science and engineering.
